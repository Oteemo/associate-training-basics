Homemade Hot Chocolate

10 mins 1 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips or 3 small blocks of dark chocolate
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • Cool Whip for topping
  • Dash of cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together milk, cocoa powder and sugar on medium heat until smooth
  2. Once smooth stir in vanilla extract, chocolate chips or chocolate blocks
  3. Pour into cup and add cool whips and cinnamon if desired

Jasmyn's Special "Not-Shepherds" Pie

1 hour 6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb hamburger helper
  • 3 skinned, boiled and chopped potatoes
  • 2 bacon strips
  • 1/2 cup of mayo
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp of white pepper
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 tsp of onion powder
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • 2 slices of cheese of your choice
  • 1 can of corn
  • chopped chives for flavour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Mash potatoes and add mayo, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and white pepper
  3. Cook bacon until crispy and mix with potatoes
  4. Mix chives into mashed potatoes
  5. Brown hamburger meat and add salt, pepper, onion and garlic powder
  6. Layer ingredients into a 9x9 baking pan
  7. Place hamburger meat, then corn, add 4 cheese slices on top of corn, and spoon in mashed potatoes
  8. Bake for 25 minutes until golden brown around the edges

Honey Glazed Salmon

30 mins 2 servings

Ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • 2 tbsp of soy sauce or teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tbsp of brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp of honey

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Mix soy/teriyaki sauce, brown sugar and honey into a small mixing bowl
  3. Place salmon on a baking sheet with aluminum foil
  4. Smear the glaze all over and on the sides of the salmon
  5. Bake for 25 mins until salmon is flaky
